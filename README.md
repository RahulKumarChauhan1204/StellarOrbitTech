StellarOrbitTech is a modern, high-fidelity conceptual landing page and operations dashboard designed for an advanced space technology firm specializing in On-Orbit Artificial Intelligence (AI).

The project showcases a futuristic, dark-mode design utilizing Glassmorphism for its UI, focusing on autonomous satellite operations, real-time telemetry analysis, and climate prediction powered by AI models deployed to space hardware (Edge AI). The narrative is centered around the transition from ground-controlled missions to the Autonomous Frontier.
